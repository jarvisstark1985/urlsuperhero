Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?
▼
We don't offer free trials. However, all paid subscribers receive 1 month of free Replit access as a subscriber bonus.
How does the referral program work?
▼
Earn 80% commission on the first payment from each customer you refer. This is a one-time commission per referral. You can refer unlimited customers and earn from each first payment. Share your unique referral link to get started.
How quickly can I register a domain?
▼
Once you complete checkout, your domain is registered instantly through our Porkbun integration. You'll have immediate access to your domain management dashboard.
Can I transfer domains from another registrar?
▼
Yes! We support domain transfers from any registrar. The process typically takes 5-7 days. Our support team will guide you through each step.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and payment through Stripe, which supports many alternative payment methods.
Is DNS management included?
▼
Yes! Full DNS management is included with every domain. Point your domain to your website, email provider, or any service you use.
Do you offer domain privacy protection?
▼
Yes, WHOIS privacy is available for most domain extensions at a small additional cost. This keeps your personal information private from public WHOIS lookups.
