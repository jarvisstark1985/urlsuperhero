Terms of Service & Subscription Agreement

1. Service Description

URL SuperHero provides AI-powered domain registration and hosting infrastructure services. Our service includes domain name registration, Cloudflare-powered hosting, SSL certificates, DNS management, and 24/7 support. All services are provided on a monthly subscription basis at $17.99 USD per month.

2. Minimum Service Commitment

All customers agree to a 90-day minimum service commitment. During this period, you may not transfer your domain to another registrar. Your domain remains held with URL SuperHero for the duration of your active subscription and the 90-day commitment period.

3. Domain Registration Policy

When you register a domain through URL SuperHero, the domain is registered with our infrastructure and cannot be transferred out for a minimum of 90 days from the start of your subscription. This policy ensures we maintain your domain registration and provide continuous hosting services.

4. Payment and Billing

Your subscription will be charged monthly at $17.99 USD. Billing occurs on the same day each month. If a payment is missed or declined, your account status changes to delinquent. All prices are subject to applicable taxes where required by law.

5. Delinquency and Account Locks

If you fail to make a payment or your payment is declined, your domain enters a locked state. The domain will remain locked with URL SuperHero for a full one (1) year or until at least 90 days of back payments have been received and processed, whichever comes first. During this lock period, the domain cannot be transferred to another registrar.

6. Domain Transfer Policy

After successfully maintaining at least 90 days of paid service and your subscription is in good standing, you may request to transfer your domain to another registrar. URL SuperHero will provide all necessary authorization codes and documentation to facilitate the transfer. Transfer requests must be made through your dashboard or support team.
